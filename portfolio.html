Saporini Italiano

Full digital transformation — chosen by an American restaurant owner

Spring Boot Kotlin Next.js PostgreSQL TypeScript
How it started

An American restaurant owner approached us to build the entire digital infrastructure for Saporini Italiano from the ground up. Not just a POS — a complete, connected system covering every part of how the restaurant operates. They chose us because we don’t just deliver software, we understand how a real business works and we build around it.

What we’re building

The system covers four pillars: online ordering so guests can place orders before they even walk in, tablet ordering at every table so guests order directly from their seat without waiting for staff, a full POS and Kitchen Display System so the kitchen always knows what’s coming in real time, and a complete inventory and stock management system so the owner always knows what’s in the magazine, what’s running low, and what needs to be ordered.

Where it stands today

The project is actively in development and already running in the restaurant. The client is highly satisfied with the direction — the system is already changing how the restaurant operates and the investor sees exactly the transformation they were looking for when they chose us.

What the system covers

Online ordering Guests order from anywhere before arriving
Table tablets Every table has its own ordering tablet
POS & Kitchen Display Real-time order flow from table to kitchen
Inventory & magazine management Full stock visibility — what’s in, what’s out, what’s low
Admin dashboard Sales, reports, menu, and staff — all in one view

Before the Bells

Digital wedding invitation platform — a German founder’s startup

View live site →
Next.js Web App UI/UX Design Custom Build
How it started

A German entrepreneur came to us with a clear vision: couples should be able to create stunning wedding invitations online without hiring a designer or dealing with print. She wanted a platform where anyone could pick a template, fill in their details, and have something beautiful ready to share in minutes. We built it from scratch, end to end.

What we built

Before the Bells lets couples choose from several curated templates across three styles — Luxury, Elegant, and Minimal — with more being added over time. Every invitation is fully personalised to the couple: Each invitation includes a live RSVP system, smart venue map, photo gallery, event timeline, and instant guest management. One shareable link contains everything. Changes go live instantly, no reprinting, no resending.

What’s next for the client

The platform launched successfully and is already serving couples. Off the back of this, the client is now planning to expand into a full event shop — covering birthdays, baby showers, gender reveals, and more. What started as a wedding invitation tool is turning into a full digital event brand. We’re part of that journey.

What the platform includes

Several curated templates Luxury, Elegant & Minimal styles — each fully personalised to the couple
Live RSVP system Guests respond instantly, tracked in real time
Smart venue map Location, parking & accommodation in one place
Photo gallery & timeline Couple photos and full event schedule
Live editing Update details any time — changes reflect instantly
“This was exactly the startup I needed. The platform is live, couples love it, and we’re already planning the next phase.”
— Founder, Before the Bells (DE)

Visual identity for a premium flower shop

Brand Design Logo Design Visual Identity
The brief

A flower shop needed a brand that felt alive — something that said fresh, premium, and natural without relying on the usual clichés. FORE needed to stand out in a market full of script fonts and pink palettes. The goal was elegant restraint: a brand that lets the flowers speak and only adds to their quality.

What we created

We built a full visual identity system grounded in natural tones and clean typography. The logo mark is minimal but distinctive. The color palette draws from botanical greens and warm neutrals, giving the brand versatility across print and digital. Business cards, stationery, and brand applications all carry the same quiet confidence.

The result

A brand that feels considered — the kind that makes customers trust the quality before they even see the product. FORE now has a visual language it can grow with, from in-store materials to social media to packaging.

Visual identity for a dog accessories brand

Brand Design Logo Design Banner Design
The brief

BOND is a dog accessories brand that takes the relationship between owners and their pets seriously — and wanted a brand to match. Not a toy shop, not a cheap pet store. A lifestyle brand for owners who buy quality for their dog the same way they would for themselves. The brief was clear: playful enough to be warm, refined enough to be premium.

What we created

We designed a visual identity that captures that balance perfectly. The logo mark has personality without being cartoonish. The color system is bold where it needs to be and restrained everywhere else. We extended the brand into a full banner design system, giving BOND a strong presence whether online or in-store. Every element is built to scale as the brand grows.

The result

A brand that dog owners immediately connect with — one that says this product was made with care. BOND has a complete visual language that works across product packaging, digital, and physical retail, ready to grow with the business.
