Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

Last updated: June 2026

1. Who we are

voidtosolved is responsible for this website and for processing the personal data you submit through it.

Contact: contact@voidtosolved.com

2. What data we collect

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • The information you provide in the message fields (project description, budget range, etc.)

We do not collect any data beyond what you voluntarily submit in the form.

3. Why we collect it

We collect this data solely to respond to your enquiry and, where relevant, to prepare a project concept for you. The legal basis is your consent, given by submitting the form (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)), and our legitimate interest in responding to business enquiries (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)).

4. How your data is processed

Form submissions are processed through Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form service. Your submission is delivered to us via their infrastructure. Formspree's own privacy policy applies to their handling of the data. We do not store your data on our own servers beyond what appears in our email inbox.

5. How long we keep your data

We retain your contact details and message for as long as is necessary to respond to your enquiry and handle any resulting business relationship. If no project proceeds, we will delete your data within 12 months of your initial contact.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at contact@voidtosolved.com.

7. Cookies and analytics

This website does not currently use cookies or analytics tracking tools. No tracking scripts are loaded.

8. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review it periodically.
